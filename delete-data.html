How to Request Deletion

If you want Hotline App to delete information you previously submitted, please send a request through the Contact us section inside the app.

  1. Open Hotline App.
  2. Tap Contact us.
  3. Mention that you want your submitted data deleted.
  4. Include enough information to identify your request.

What Data Can Be Deleted

  • Contact us messages
  • Add Number submissions
  • Business plan requests
  • Submitted name and phone details associated with those requests

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited internal records if required for fraud prevention, legal compliance, or service integrity. If retained, this will be limited to what is reasonably necessary.

Processing Time

We aim to review and process deletion requests within a reasonable time after receiving them.

This page applies to Hotline App and data submitted through the app's feedback and business inquiry forms.
