Hotline App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Hotline App does not require account registration for normal use. We only collect limited information when users choose to submit it through the app.

  • Add Number submissions
  • Contact us messages and suggestions
  • Business plan requests

Submitted information may include:

  • Name
  • Business or organization name
  • Phone or WhatsApp number
  • Hotline number
  • Message or request details

2. How We Use Information

We use submitted information only to:

  • Review hotline and number submissions
  • Improve the app database
  • Respond to user suggestions or inquiries
  • Review and follow up on business plan requests

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Submitted information is not shared with advertisers or third-party analytics providers. Limited service providers may process data only as needed to operate the app, such as hosting and email delivery services.

4. Data Retention

Requests, suggestions, and business inquiries may be stored in our backend systems for review, management, and service improvement.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect submitted information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Hotline App is not specifically directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

You may use the app's browsing and search features without providing personal information. If you wish to request deletion of submitted data, you can contact us through the app.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the Contact us section inside Hotline App.

Hotline App is committed to transparency, responsible data handling, and a simple user experience.
